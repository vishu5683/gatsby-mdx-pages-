How to connect Sternet Smart Devices with Google Assistant



Step 1: Before You Set Up Google Assistant



Ensure that Sternet Smart device is paired with Internet

  • To start pairing, follow this guide here
  • Your Sternet Smart account will be used to link your device to Alexa. Keep your login credentials handy.

Ensure that your Google Assistant is configured

  • Google Home app is required to complete the linking process.
  • To setup your Google Home device, follow this guide here


Step 2: Open the Google Home app and go to the “Devices” tab and then select “+ Add” button, then “Works with Google”




Step 3: Search for Sternet Smart in the list of devices and tap on “Sternet Smart”




Step 4: Enter your Sternet Smart cloud email and password (the same as what you use in the Sternet Smart app)




Step 5: Your devices will now appear in the Google Home app! It’s time to start telling The Google Assistant to control your lights!




What can I say to Google Assistant (Google Home)?

Ex- Device named- Bedroom Light

  • Ok google, turn on/off bedroom light
  • Ok google, dim bedroom light
  • Ok google, brighten bedroom light
  • Ok google, set bedroom light to 80%
  • Ok google, turn bedroom light to white
